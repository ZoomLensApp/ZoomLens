# Privacy Policy

ZoomLens works locally on your computer.

ZoomLens does not collect telemetry, record your screen, take screenshots, or send screen contents anywhere.

## What ZoomLens stores

ZoomLens stores local settings in `config.ini`, such as:

- magnifier mode
- zoom level
- lens size
- selected monitors
- hotkeys
- interface language

This file stays on your computer.

## Network access

ZoomLens does not require an internet connection for its magnifier features.

The app may open external links when you click buttons such as:

- Check for updates
- GitHub Issues
- Support links

Opening those links uses your default browser.

## Feedback and diagnostics

If you choose to report a bug, you may copy diagnostics from the app and include them in a GitHub issue or message.

Diagnostics are copied only when you click the button. They are not sent automatically.

## Screen contents

ZoomLens does not upload, transmit, or store your screen contents.

All magnification happens locally through Windows APIs.
